**Critical invariant (orchestrator invariant #3):** The sha256 canonical form — `json.dumps(rows, sort_keys=True, separators=(",", ":"), default=_json_default)` — MUST match Plan 02-01's `json_repo.py` byte-for-byte. Any divergence makes every client-side fetch fail with "sha256 mismatch" and the app won't start.

**Runtime verification at implementation time (orchestrator resolution #4):** Before locking the TABLES tuple at the 9-table count, the executor MUST query Supabase once (via a one-off script) to confirm whether `champion_stats` rows already carry a `name` field.
